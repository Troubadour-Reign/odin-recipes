Description

Our milchreis is the perfect alternative to your morning oatmeal or a great option for a warm dessert.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix the rice, sugar, and salt in a large saucepan, pour in milk.
  2. Place the mixture over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring often.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes, or until the rice is soft and the mixture becomes thick. Stir often.
  4. Optional. A dollop of fruit compote, fresh fruit, or your favorite combination of toppings makes this a really special sweet treat.
