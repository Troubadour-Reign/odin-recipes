Lasagna

Description

This recipe uses simple ingredients that you likely have on hand, and it packs big flavor!

It reheats well and can be made ahead of time. You can freeze lasagna either before or after baking.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
